Computer :

Neck, shoulders, wrists: inadequate ergonomics. Eye fatigue and headaches: screens too close or too bright. Cognitive stress: information overload, permanent multitasking. Mental fatigue: prolonged concentration without breaks. Risks related to immobility: lower back pain, circulatory disorders.
