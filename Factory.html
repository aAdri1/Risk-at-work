Factory :

Mechanical accidents: machines, presses, conveyors. Cuts and crushes: handling of tools, metal parts. Intense noise: serious auditory risks. Exposure to hazardous substances: vapors, solvents, dust. Offloads: handling, storage at height. Electrical risks. Repetitive work: muscle fatigue. Working in shifts or at night: fatigue, decreased alertness.
